I'm a fourth-year undergraduate at the University of Washington studying computer science. At the UW, I have been fortunate to work with [Shwetak Patel](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~shwetak/) and [Noah Smith](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~nasmith/). My research interests are at the intersections of NLP and HCI, with a focus on applications in health. I have also worked on building ubiquitous computing sensing tools for healthcare and novel iteractions.
